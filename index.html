What we do

As a group we are developing an cargo application that will match people who want their cargo to be carried and couriers who will deliver these cargos to their destination.

Fast, Efficient, Cheap

Wheelancer aims to maintain the good qualities of well-known cargo companies while being cheap.

Security and Safety

While using Wheelancer, the cargos will be carried in a safe and secure way.

Platform

Wheelancer will be available on Android Marshmallow 6.1 and newer versions when finished.

Easy and User Friendly

The interface of the Wheelancer will be easy to understand and use for with respect to all users.
